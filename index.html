The vision

A mobile operating environment, not just a gadget.

Most gear solves one problem at a time. The 3dvr Nomad System is meant to solve the whole chain: carry your essentials, deploy a workspace, keep your devices powered, and have a place to rest.

⚙️

Designed from first principles

Built around portability, repairability, and real-world use instead of desk-bound assumptions.

🌍

Works anywhere

Train, van, hotel, tent site, co-working space, or coffee shop. Your environment moves with you.

🧠

Software-defined lifestyle

Tie the hardware together with 3dvr software, browser tools, sync, and AI-assisted workflows.

The modules

Five pieces. One ecosystem.

Each part should be useful on its own, but stronger together. Start with existing gear, then refine toward a custom 3dvr product line.

🎒

Backpack — the base unit

Carries the whole system and eventually becomes part of the desk structure. Houses power, storage, cable routing, and your travel-ready tech stack.

Hard + soft hybrid Battery storage Solar-ready
🏕️

Tent — the shelter layer

Lightweight, fast to deploy, and designed for living with devices. Internal hanging points, warm lighting, airflow, and a compact footprint make it part of the working system.

Ultralight Device-friendly Low-light living
🧍

Desk — the deployable workspace

A foldable or telescoping frame that creates an instant sit/stand setup. Meant for transit, hotel rooms, temporary offices, and field work.

Sit/stand Fast deploy Modular mounts
💻

Laptop — the compute core

Start with a repairable laptop or compact SBC stack. Eventually evolve toward a 3dvr-designed open-source computer built for mobility, field serviceability, and cloud-linked workflows.

Repairable Linux-friendly Cloud + local
📱

Phone — the control center

Your uplink, dashboard, scanner, camera, communication hub, and backup interface. The phone is what makes the whole system feel alive while moving.

Dockable Hotspot-ready Field control
Why it matters

For people building a life outside the default template.

The future is not only remote work. It is mobile work, independent work, open work, and resilient work. The 3dvr Nomad System is for creators who want to stay capable without being locked into fragile, expensive, office-only infrastructure.

  • Digital nomads who want a serious setup, not just a laptop sleeve.
  • Builders, hackers, artists, and field operators who work between places.
  • People who want repairable gear and modular systems instead of disposable devices.
  • Anyone who sees life, shelter, mobility, and computing as one integrated design problem.

“Your office isn’t a place. Your system is.”

3dvr is about turning mobility, grit, and open-source thinking into something beautiful, useful, and eventually productized.
